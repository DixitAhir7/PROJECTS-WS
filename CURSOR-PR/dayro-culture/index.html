ABOUT

Dayro is a traditional form of Gujarati folk music and poetry that has been passed down through generations. It's a unique blend of music, poetry, and storytelling that reflects the rich cultural heritage of Gujarat.

The art form typically involves a group of performers who sing and recite poetry, often accompanied by traditional musical instruments.
